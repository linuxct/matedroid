MateDroid is a native Android application for viewing Tesla vehicle data from your self-hosted Teslamate instance via TeslamateApi.

<b>Features:</b>

• <b>Dashboard</b> - Real-time vehicle status at a glance with 3D car image matching your vehicle's color and wheels
• <b>Stats for Nerds</b> - Tap car image for advanced statistics: records, extremes, AC/DC ratio and much more!
• <b>Charging History</b> - View all charging sessions with statistics and charts
• <b>Charge Details</b> - Interactive map and detailed power/voltage/temperature charts
• <b>Drive History</b> - Track trips with efficiency metrics and route visualization
• <b>Battery Health</b> - Monitor battery degradation over time
• <b>Software Updates</b> - Track update history
• <b>Car color based themes</b> - Light/dark themes with palette based on your car color

<b>Requirements:</b>

• Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher
• A running Teslamate instance (https://github.com/adriankumpf/teslamate)
• TeslamateApi deployed and accessible (https://github.com/tobiasehlert/teslamateapi)

<b>Privacy:</b>

MateDroid connects only to your self-hosted server. No data is sent to third parties. Your Tesla data stays under your control.
